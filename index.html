Let's Go Cycling

The best rides happen on two wheels!

Cycling is not only a sport, it can be a lifestyle!

After some time on the bike, you may find that the only downside to cycling is that it can be addictive. I joined a cycling club in May 2019 for the intention of getting used to my new bike. Never did I know that it would change my life! I have made lots of new friends, ridden Audax's, been on cycling adventures and this is just the beginning. I'm looking forward to the adventures to come!

Apps that make cycling even more fun!

There are many apps you can use to make to keep track of your rides, get inspiration, plan your next adventure and much more. Here are just a few examples.

Strava

The #1 app for cyclists and runners. It is the social network for athletes. You can upload your routes from your bike computer to Strava and let other people see you rides.
Visit Strava

Wahoo

The Wahoo GPS bike computer give you the confidence to cycle further and further! Tell it where you want to go and it will show you the way.
Visit Wahoo!

Komoot

Use Komoot to get inspiration and plan your routes in advance! Type in where you want to cycle, and it will plan the route for you. Transfer the route to your bike computer and off you go!!
Visit Komoot
